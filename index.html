Georgy Zhukov

Minister of Defence Union of Soviet Socialist Republics

Here is a timeline of Georgiy Zhukov's life:

  • December 2, 1896 - Birth of Georgy Zhukov
  • 1903 - Begins Elementary School
  • 1940 - Zhukov delivers report "the character of contemporary offensive operations"
  • 1941 - Zhukov and Timoshenko meet Stalin
  • 1943 - Zhukov cables Stalin that Polar Star should be limited to capturing Staraya Russa
  • 1956 Flies to Warsaw with Konev
  • 1965 - Divorces Alexandra Dievna. Marries Galina Semonova
  • June 18, 1974 - Moscow - Georgy Zhukov dies in the Kremlin Hospital, aged 77
